What is Forex Trading?

Forex trading, also known as foreign exchange trading or currency trading, is the buying and selling of currencies in the foreign exchange market with the aim of making a profit.

The forex market is the largest and most liquid financial market in the world, with trillions of dollars traded every day.

Benefits of Forex Trading

  • High liquidity
  • 24-hour market
  • Low transaction costs
  • Ability to profit from both rising and falling markets
  • Access to leverage
